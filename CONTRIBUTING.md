# Contributing to PsicoEval

Thank you for your interest in contributing to PsicoEval! This document provides guidelines for contributions.

## 🚧 Current Status

PsicoEval is currently in MVP development. We're not accepting external contributions until after the initial release, but we appreciate your interest!

## 📋 Future Contribution Areas

After MVP launch, we'll welcome contributions in:
- Additional validated psychological instruments
- UI/UX improvements
- Accessibility enhancements
- Documentation improvements
- Bug fixes and performance optimizations
- Internationalization (i18n)

## 🔐 Important Note

This platform handles sensitive mental health data. All contributions must:
- Follow HIPAA compliance guidelines
- Maintain data privacy and security standards
- Include appropriate tests
- Be reviewed by clinical professionals (for instrument-related changes)

## 📧 Contact

For questions or suggestions, please open an issue on GitHub.
